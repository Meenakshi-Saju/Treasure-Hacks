Sport (or sports) is all forms of usually competitive physical activity which, through casual or organised participation, aim to use, maintain or improve physical ability and skills while providing entertainment to participants, and in some cases, spectators. There are hundreds of sports, ranging from single-player competitions to those with hundreds of people playing at the same time, either in teams or as individuals. Many competitors may compete concurrently or sequentially in certain sports, such as racing, with one winner; in others, the contest (a match) is between two teams, each aiming to outperform the other. Some sports allow for a "tie" or "draw," in which no single winner is determined; others have tie-breaking procedures in place to ensure that one winner and one loser are determined. In a tournament, a number of competitions may be held in order to choose a champion. Many sports leagues select an annual champion by scheduling games during a regular season, followed by playoffs in certain circumstances.
